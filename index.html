Business Law

Business law encompasses legal issues related to running a business, including contracts, liability, intellectual property, and compliance with regulations.

Criminal Law

Criminal law deals with offenses against the state, defining crimes, setting penalties, and ensuring justice through prosecution and defense..

Child Support

Child support involves financial contributions from a non-custodial parent to help cover the costs of raising a child after separation.

Education Law

Education law governs the legal aspects of schooling, including students' rights, school policies, special education, and educational standards and compliance.

Divorce Law

Divorce law manages the legal dissolution of marriage, addressing issues like asset division, child custody, support, and spousal maintenance.

Tax Law

Tax law governs the assessment and collection of taxes, including income, property, and corporate taxes, ensuring compliance with tax regulations.

Are application fee waivers available?

Yes, application fee waivers are available for eligible individuals. You may qualify based on financial need, specific criteria, or participation in certain programs. Please check the eligibility requirements and application process on our website.

What are the eligibility criteria for an application fee waiver?

To be eligible for an application fee waiver, you typically need to demonstrate financial hardship or meet specific criteria such as participation in government assistance programs, being a member of a qualifying organization, or meeting income thresholds. Detailed eligibility requirements can be found on our website.

How do I apply for a fee waiver?

To apply for a fee waiver, you will need to complete the fee waiver request form available on our website and submit it along with any required documentation. The review process may take a few days, and you will be notified of the decision via email or we can call us anytime .

Can I receive a refund if I already paid the application fee but now qualify for a waiver?

Unfortunately, application fees are non-refundable, even if you later qualify for a waiver. It's important to apply for the waiver before submitting your application to avoid unnecessary charges.
